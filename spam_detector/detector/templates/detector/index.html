⚙️ How It Works

1
TF-IDF Vectorization
Your text is transformed into a sparse numerical vector using Term Frequency–Inverse Document Frequency, capturing word importance across 276,915 features.
2
Naive Bayes Classification
MultinomialNB applies Bayes' theorem to compute posterior probabilities for each class, leveraging the training distribution of 83,448 labeled emails.
3
Confidence Score
The model returns the probability for each class. The higher the confidence, the more certain the model is about its prediction.
